Trip of the Season

Annapurna Circuit, Nepal

One of the world's great high-altitude walks — 14 days through terraced villages, prayer-flag passes, and mornings that make every step worthwhile. Guided by locals who know every stone on the trail.

  • Day 1–2Pokhara arrival — lakeside acclimatisation & gear check
  • Day 3–6Jomsom to Manang — high desert valleys, apple orchards
  • Day 7–9Thorong La Pass (5,416m) — the unforgettable summit
  • Day 10–14Descent & Muktinath — sacred temples, hot springs, celebration
